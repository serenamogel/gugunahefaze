Artist Who Painted Chris Jericho &amp; Don Callis Art On AEW Dynamite Comes Forward

AEW Dynamite featured plenty of interesting segments this week. Don Callis and Chris Jericho continued their storyline this week, and it also included quite a work of art. Now, we know who was behind that portrait.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite this week.

Mel Coleman tweeted out that they are the artist responsible for that Don Callis and Chris Jericho artwork. As Bad News Brown looked down upon them, AEW fans were in shock and awe when the painting was revealed.

The artist behind that work of art tweeted out a screenshot of the painting on AEW Dynamite this week. It was quite an artistic achievement, as Mel Coleman also stated that Don Callis deserves the best.

Mr.Callis and the Don Family deserve nothing but the best, which is why he comes to me.

We will have to see what’s next for Chris Jericho and Don Callis’ storyline in AEW. After all, the Jericho Appreciation Society doesn’t really appreciate this new alliance. It seems that Jericho might only have Callis left as a friend in a matter of time.

Tony Khan’s roster is obviously having a lot of fun with this storyline. It seems that it could lead to a big tag team match down the line, but only time will tell how it all pans out. If anything, we got quite a painting out of the deal.
