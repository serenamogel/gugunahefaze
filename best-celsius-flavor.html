Best Celsius Flavor: We Tasted and Ranked All 18 Celsius Flavors

Finding the best Celsius flavor can be kind of complicated. First of all, Celsius—the self-proclaimed “healthy energy” drink—comes in a lot of flavors. We attempted to be exhaustive and tried 18. Prolificacy aside, some Celsius flavors aren’t named for naturally occurring flavors. Yes, you have your Orange and Grape, but there’s a whole Celsius line named for ~ vibes ~ and, frankly, I don’t know what a “fantasy vibe” is supposed to taste like. (I mean, I can come up with some things, but I can’t say that’s what they were going for.)

But here’s the good news about the many, many flavors of Celsius: A lot of them taste very good. Celsius’s whole thing is that it’s meant to be a pre-workout drink. I don’t work out, per se, but taste testing food several days a week can sure feel like an endurance sport, and in general I found Celsius’ drinks to have a really pleasant, refreshing effervescence. I also like that they’re zero sugar and low cal but don’t taste overwhelmingly like artificial sweeteners (for the most part, anyway). But not every Celsius flavor is great. Before you head to the gym with a can, check out our ranking.

Celsius!

Celsius Sparkling Grape RushCelsius

When you set out to find the best Celsius flavor, it’s inevitable that one will be the worst. Congrats, Sparkling Grape Rush! You did it! This tasted like root beer to me and I can’t explain why. Our intern, Kota Lee, said it made their mouth and throat dry, which is definitely not what you want before you work out.

Celsius!

Celsius Sparkling OrangeCelsius

Is it boring that the best Celsius flavor is … just plain old orange? Maybe, but it just tastes so good. If you love the mild, not-too-tart flavor of canned mandarin oranges, you’ll love this. It’s flavorful. It’s sweet. The carbonation makes it taste like a delicious orange soda, but you get a punch of caffeine, too. This is a crowd pleaser, and that’s part of what makes it the best Celsius flavor you can buy. Sometimes a classic is a classic for a reason!
