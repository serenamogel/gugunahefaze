C. J. Uzomah contract, salary and net worth explored

Christopher James Uzomah, who was born on January 14, 1993, is a tight end for the National Football League’s New York Jets (NFL).

He played college football at Auburn. In the fifth round of the 2015 NFL Draft, the Cincinnati Bengals picked him up.

On March 3, 2015, he went to Auburn’s pro day with Cameron Artis-Payne, Angelo Blackson, Quan Bray, Sammie Coates, Reese Dismukes, Corey Grant, Nick Marshall, Brandon King, Jermaine Whitehead, Gabe Wright, Trovon Reed, and nine other teammates.

Uzomah did all the positional drills and combine tests in front of scouts and people from all 32 NFL teams.

Most NFL draft experts and analysts thought he would be taken in the sixth or seventh round or could be a priority free agent. He was the 12th best tight end prospect in the draft, according to NFLDraftScout.com.
