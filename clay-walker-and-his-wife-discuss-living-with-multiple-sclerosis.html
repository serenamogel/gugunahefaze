Clay Walker and His Wife Discuss Living with Multiple Sclerosis

Clay Walker and his model wife, Jessica Craig, parents of seven children, are seemingly invincible. At the same time, juggling their roles as career managers and parents of seven children, Walker and his wife deal with his multiple sclerosis. In 1996, Walker was diagnosed with multiple sclerosis. Within the next eight years, he was told he would be unable to walk and would die.

When he started experiencing the symptoms, the singer knew something was wrong, and the diagnosis did not come as a surprise. In spite of this, Craig was still able to fall in love with the man and bring joy into his life. In the end, Walker’s wife was also his caregiver. Walker’s diagnosis was the subject of an interview with Parade, in which the couple discussed their experiences with it.

He relies on his wife to take care of him.

Walker, who was engaged to be married, confessed that he was afraid he would bring his illness to bear on his future wife and children. This led to a lengthy discussion between the two. According to him, he and Craig got along well because Craig was upfront and honest with him. Although she had no prior knowledge of the subject, the model was unafraid to face it head-on.

She explained that “eating right, getting enough exercise, and taking his medication” were the cornerstones of Craig’s daily regimen for the singer. His diet was organic, and he worked out with his family. The two of them enjoy running around the yard together. She also shared an important piece of advice. She emphasized the importance of having a group of people to lean on. To ensure that Walker was getting his workouts while on the road, she would call up his bandmates and ask them if they could help her out.

Children of Clay Walker

Also, in the interview, Craig revealed that she had always wanted to have children. In January 2021, Christiaan Michael Walker, the couple’s youngest child, was born. William Clayton Walker, Walker’s seventh child, has three other children with Craig: Ezra Stephen, 3, Elijah Craig, 7, and William Clayton, 12, as well as 11-year-old Mary Elizabeth. Skylor, 21, and Maclay, 25, are his stepdaughters from a previous marriage. As the father of seven children, the man revealed to People that he and Craig were comforted by the fact that they could rely on the support of their children.

Even his then 10-year-old daughter, Mary Elizabeth, was ecstatic about the impending arrival of a new boy into the family, he remarked. The singer gushed about his wife’s desire to become a mother again, saying that she was born to be a mother. Everyone around her knows she’s a saint, and I can attest to that. ” “It’s the most difficult job on the planet,” he said. According to him, there is no more selfless lifestyle than that of a mother.

Clay Walker Wiki

Clay Walker is an American country singer who has four platinum solo albums to her credit. He is best known for his songs “Dreaming with My Eyes Open” and “If I Could Make a Living.”

When he was a teenager, he worked at a Super 8 Hotel and had one of his songs played on a local radio station. After high school, he worked at a Goodyear Tire plant.

Since 1993, he has had six number-one singles, including “What’s it to You?”
From 1992 to 2003, he was married to Lori Jayne Lampson. They had two daughters together. With his second wife, Jessica Craig, whom he married in 2007, he had three sons and a daughter. In January 2021, their fifth child was born.
