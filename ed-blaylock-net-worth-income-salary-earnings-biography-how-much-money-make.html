Ed Blaylock Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Ed Blaylock (born June 10, 1946 in Dallas, Texas) is an American radio announcer and video game and anime voice actor. From Fullmetal Alchemist and Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood, he played Führer King Bradley. Funimation voice performers Mike McFarland and Jerry Jewell are the only active ADR Directors who have cast Ed Blaylock in recent dubs. He is now the daily evening announcer for WRR Classical 101.FM Dallas.
