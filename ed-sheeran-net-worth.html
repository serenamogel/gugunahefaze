Highlights

Early Life

Edward Christopher Sheeran was born in Halifax, England on February 17, 1991, and grew up in Framlingham. He discovered his passion for music at a young age, starting with singing in a local choir before even attending kindergarten. Sheeran then learned to play the guitar and began writing his own songs while in high school. His talent and dedication eventually led him to be accepted into the National Youth Theatre.

Career

Sheeran’s music career began in 2004 when he started recording his own music independently. He gained a small following with performances at smaller venues in London and collaborations with various artists. In 2011, he released his debut studio album, ‘+’, which rose to the top of charts and propelled him into mainstream success.

His subsequent albums, ‘x’ (Multiply) in 2014 and ‘÷’ (Divide) in 2017, further solidified his position as one of the most talented and successful artists of his generation. Sheeran’s ability to blend different genres and connect emotionally with his audience has been a key component of his appeal.

Personal Life
