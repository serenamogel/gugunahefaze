Fred Armisen Net Worth

Fred Armisen is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer, and musician born on December 4, 1966 in Hattiesburg, MS. He is best known for his work on Saturday Night Live, where he became known for his impression of President Barack Obama. He and Carrie Brownstein co-created the Emmy-nominated IFC sketch series Portlandia, which premiered in 2011. He also voiced the character Brainy Smurf in the 2011 film The Smurfs.

About

Saturday Night Live cast member who became known for his impression of President Barack Obama. He and Carrie Brownstein co-created the Emmy-nominated IFC Sketch series Portlandia, which premiered in 2011. He voiced the character Brainy Smurf in the 2011 film The Smurfs.

Before Fame

He attended New York's School of Visual Arts and later pursued a career as a rock Drummer. He began his comedy career by performing on Late Night with Conan O'Brien and Crank Yankers.

Trivia

He became known for his comedic impersonations of such celebrities as Steve Jobs and Prince.

Family Life

He was married for six years to British singer and Songwriter Sally Timms. He married Actress Elisabeth Moss in 2009; the couple divorced in 2011. In 2014, he began a relationship with Orange Is the New Black star Natasha Lyonne.

Associated With

He acted opposite Will Ferrell on Saturday Night Live and in the hit comedy film Anchorman.
