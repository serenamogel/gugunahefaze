When Will Jacqueline Macinnes Wood Welcome Her Fourth Baby?

Since their July 2018 wedding, the daytime actress has been happily raising three sons. Her eldest son Rise (born March 2019), Lenix (born February 2021) and Brando (born May 2022). Recently Jacqueline announced that she is expecting her fourth child and below you will read more about her pregnancy news.

What is Jacqueline Macinnes Wood’s Delivery Date?

On the set of the daytime talk show “The Talk” on Monday, April 17th, Jacqueline MacInnes Wood not only celebrated her birthday and announced she is pregnant with her fourth child. She also promoted the forthcoming 9000th episode of her show, “The Bold and the Beautiful.”

Jacqueline MacInnes Wood who plays Steffy on CBS’s The Bold and the Beautiful announced on a beautiful day that she is having her fourth child. The news had been the subject of much speculation online.

On the episode of The Talk on April 17, Wood made the announcement and talked about the 9,000th episode of the CBS soap opera which is broadcast today April 18.

On Monday’s episode of The Talk, Jacqueline discussed her legacy with the hosts and filled them in on family life. Sheryl Underwood, Akbar Gbaja-Biamila, Jerry O’Connell, Natalie Morales and Amanda Kloot of CBS all wanted to know when Jacqueline will show up on the special episode of the soap opera.

While enjoying a specially-decorated cake presented by the show’s hosts, Wood said:

“I had a great time because all I wanted to was to sleep and eat, The other thing is I couldn’t party hard because I’m pregnant again.”

After Jacqueline revealed the information, there was a thunderous ovation from the audience and program moderators and congratulations from Akbar. She continued:

“So all I wanted for my birthday was just to eat and sleep but when I’m not pregnant, I am a good time.”

Last year, Jacqueline welcomed her third child into the world. In 2022, Jacqueline discussed her methods of concealing her pregnancy with ET Canada. In a behind-the-scenes video, she demonstrated one of the strategies.

She said:

“See, I’m pregnant, It’s hilarious how when I use my hands to communicate”.
