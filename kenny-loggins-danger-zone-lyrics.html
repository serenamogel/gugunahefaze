Kenny Loggins Danger Zone Lyrics

“Danger Zone Lyrics” is a song recorded by American singer-songwriter Kenny Loggins. It was written by Giorgio Moroder and Tom Whitlock, and produced by Moroder.

About

Kenny Loggins

Kenny Loggins is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist. He was born on January 7, 1948, in Everett, Washington. Loggins began his musical career as a member of the group Loggins and Messina in the 1970s, but he later went solo.

He’s best known for his hit songs such as “Footloose,” “Danger Zone,” “I’m Alright,” and “Danny’s Song.” Loggins has also written and recorded music for numerous movies, including Top Gun and Caddyshack.

Over the course of his career, he has won two Grammy Awards and received several other nominations.
