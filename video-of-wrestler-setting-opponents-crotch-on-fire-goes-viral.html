Video Of Wrestler Setting Opponents Crotch On Fire Goes Viral

Deathmatch wrestling has received a spotlight with Dark Side Of The Ring focusing on names like Nick Gage and New Jack (RIP). Pro Wrestling Trainwreck held a show recently that featured one spot which got a ton of attention.

A video clip of one wrestler setting another on fire has gone viral. One video received over 1 million views and tons of responses. This was not just any normal spot where a wrestler sets his opponent on fire either.

This clip shows a wrestler allowing his opponent set his crotch on fire. Then the man on fire remained still as his opponent fetched a weed wacker to nail him one more time right in the bathing suit area.

This was a hard spot for a lot of people to watch, to say the least. It also received a ton of criticism from fans who don’t appreciate this brand of deathmatch wrestling. It was certainly a crazy clip. Needless to say, Jim Cornette was tagged in this video many times.
