Why Did Pewdiepie Move To Japan With His Family? Is He Going To Live There With His Wife Marzia?

PewDiePie, the famous Swedish YouTuber, has moved to Japan, revealing the news on his YouTube channel over two and a half years after originally declaring his plan.

PewDiePie, the popular YouTuber, shares an emotional video after moving to Japan. The YouTuber’s wish to live in Japan has finally been fulfilled. He shared his plans for moving to Japan in his YouTube video 2 and a half years ago. However, due to the pandemic, it was not possible for him to move.

Why Did PewDiePie Move To Japan With His Family?

Felix Kjellberg, better known as PewDiePie, is a Swedish YouTuber. He recently took to his YouTube channel to announce that he along with his wife and pets have moved to Japan. He lived in the UK for almost 10 years before moving to Japan.

The Youtuber has always considered moving to another nation, specifically Japan. PewDiePie chose Japan because of the country and its culture, and he had always admired and expressed his love for Japan. He has traveled to Japan several times with his wife Marzia.

Although he had announced that he was planning to move to Japan 2 years ago, PewDiePie just moved in 2022. His plans were put on hold for two years due to the pandemic.

However, on 9th May 2022,  PewDiePie shared the big news in a video that he and his wife Marzia, as well as their two pugs, have already moved to Japan.

Is He Going To Live There With His Wife Marzia?

Looks like the world’s famous YouTuber, PewDiePie’s wish of living in Japan has finally come true. The YouTuber has announced that he has arrived in his dreamland in Japan with his wife, Marzia, and two dogs. He is living at his house in Japan with his wife Marzia and plans to live in the country for quite some time.

After living in the UK for 10 years, the Swedish YouTuber made his decision to move to Japan in 2019. The YouTuber has also shared a lot of travel vlogs from his yearly vacations to Japan, which he openly admits he adores.

PewDiePie On Changing His House

PewDiePie had surprised his fans by sharing the news that he bought a house in Japan in 2019 However, the fans were not that shocked given the YouTuber’s love for Japan and Japanese culture.

He has finally moved into his house in Japan with his wife Marzia and two pugs. This is the same house that was robbed only a few months after PewDiePie had purchased it.

Meanwhile, his fans and followers are really happy and excited to see the Youtuber and his family living in his dream place.
